Steam Account Manager

The Spiritual Successor to Steam Account Swicher, Steam Account Manager allows easy to manage steam account with a easy GUI, logout of steam with an easy 1 click or switch accounts with a double click. No Options To configure.

Features include:

  • Logout of steam and switch to unlimited accounts whenever you won't
  • easy to use GUI
  • daily updates
  • military grade password protected (password CryptoCoded stored locally)
